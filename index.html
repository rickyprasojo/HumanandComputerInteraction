YBIKE HISTORY

YBIKE is a motorcycle dealer formed on December 30, 2016 and based in Jakarta, Indonesia. YBIKE sells motorcycles from 2 famous brands of motorcycle: Vixian and Kawasako. The dealer is led by Carvin Joestar. The products are kept inside a secure warehouse and covered with a large fabric to keep them clean of dusts.YBIKE was established to fulfill people's desires so that they can have a dream vehicle. With YBIKE, everyone is able to realize their dreams.
